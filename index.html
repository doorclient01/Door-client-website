What's inside

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Door Client wraps the parts of the modded experience that actually matter into one clean launcher — so setup takes seconds, not evenings.

01

Performance boost

Optimized rendering and chunk handling for noticeably smoother, higher FPS gameplay out of the box.

02

One-click mod install

Browse, install, and update mods from inside the launcher — no manual file dragging, no version guesswork.

03

Custom HUD & cosmetics

Personalize your overlay, cape, and in-game look with options that stay out of your way while you play.

04

Own launcher

A dedicated launcher handles accounts, versions, and updates — Door Client stays out of your Minecraft folder.

05

Auto updates

New builds roll out automatically, so you're always running the latest, most stable version of the client.

06

Built by players

Made and maintained by a small team that actually plays with it daily — features ship because we wanted them too.
